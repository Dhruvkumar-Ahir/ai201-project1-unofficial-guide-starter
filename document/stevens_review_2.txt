According to the Yelp page, Stevens Institute of Technology is generally viewed as a strong engineering-focused university with a scenic campus overlooking the Manhattan skyline in Hoboken, New Jersey. Many reviewers praised the location, noting that New York City is easily accessible by bus, ferry, and public transportation, making the university attractive for students seeking both academic opportunities and access to a major metropolitan area. Several former students highlighted the quality of the engineering and science programs, the availability of internships, strong career prospects after graduation, and the sense of community among students and faculty. Reviewers also mentioned campus amenities such as fitness facilities, pools, tennis courts, cafés, and student housing, contributing to a traditional college experience in an urban setting.

At the same time, the reviews reveal a mixed overall experience. Some graduate students expressed frustration with administrative support, particularly regarding academic advising, degree requirements, and communication from university offices. Others criticized aspects of the curriculum, claiming that certain engineering courses emphasized theory more than practical industry skills. A few reviewers also raised concerns about scholarship policies, billing issues, and negative experiences with specific staff members. Despite these criticisms, several alumni stated that the education they received helped them build valuable skills, secure internships, and prepare for successful careers.
